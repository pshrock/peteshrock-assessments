Character & Success Development

Assess your development across the four core COREsuccess™ dimensions

1. When faced with a choice between doing what's easy and doing what's right, how do you typically respond?
2. How do you handle situations when you make mistakes or things don't go as planned?
3. When you experience significant challenges or setbacks, how do you typically respond?
4. How do you approach your goals and standards for yourself in school, work, or activities?
5. How do you treat people who are different from you or who may not be able to help you in return?
6. When working in group settings (team, family, class), how do you contribute to the overall success?
